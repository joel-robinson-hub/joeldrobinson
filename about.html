Who I Am

I'm Joel D. Robinson, CPA, a senior finance and technology professional with a deep background in FP&A, analytics, automation, and operational leadership. My experience spans SaaS, cloud infrastructure, and data-intensive organizations, with a focus on building systems, processes, and tools that scale.

Professional Background

Throughout my career, I’ve served in leadership roles including Vice President of FP&A and strategic finance leader at high-growth companies. My professional strengths include:

  • Advanced financial modeling & forecasting
  • Data engineering & analytics automation
  • Cloud technology & API integrations
  • Cross-functional leadership with GTM, product, and operations
  • Building automated reporting, metrics, and business intelligence systems

Technical Focus

In addition to finance and operations leadership, I build software systems, automation pipelines, notifications tools, and web applications as personal projects. My technical work includes:

  • API integrations and data ingestion pipelines

  • Web applications with React, Firebase, and serverless architecture
  • Automated alert/notification systems with strict compliance controls

Personal Interests

Outside of work, I enjoy software building, automation experiments, Disneyland data projects, drone flying, and strategic board games.
